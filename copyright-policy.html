Copyright

The Journal of Technology, Engineering, Architecture and Design (JTEAD) respects the intellectual property rights of authors and readers, and is committed to publishing content in accordance with applicable copyright laws and ethical standards.

1

Copyright Ownership

Authors retain the copyright to their work. By publishing in JTEAD, authors grant the journal the right of first publication and a non-exclusive license to publish, reproduce, distribute, and make their work publicly available in all formats and media.

2

Author Permission and Publication Rights

Authors retain ownership of their work. By publishing in JTEAD, authors grant the journal permission to publish, archive, and disseminate the accepted article through its official platforms. Specific licensing terms will be finalized once the journal adopts an official copyright and open-access policy.

3

Author Responsibilities

Authors are responsible for obtaining permission to reproduce any copyrighted material (including figures, tables, or large text excerpts) from other sources and must give appropriate credit to the original source.

4

Third-Party Material

JTEAD is not responsible for the content of copyrighted material included in articles submitted by authors. Authors must ensure that they have the right to use such material in their work.

5

Copyright Infringement

If you believe that any content on this site infringes your copyright, please contact the Editorial Office. We will address all legitimate claims in accordance with applicable laws and take appropriate action.

This policy is subject to change without prior notice. Please check this page regularly for updates.

Our Commitment

JTEAD is committed to upholding the highest standards of publication ethics and to protecting the rights of authors, readers, and the scholarly community.

Need Assistance?

For any questions related to copyright or reuse of content published in JTEAD, please contact the Editorial Office.
