Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information the Journal of Technology, Engineering, Architecture and Design (JTEAD) collects through this website, and how it is used and protected.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This is a general template policy and has not yet been reviewed by University of San Agustin's legal/compliance office. Have it formally reviewed before relying on it as a binding policy.

Jump to: Information We Collect How We Use Your Information Cookies and Tracking Data Sharing Data Retention and Security Your Rights Changes to This Policy Contact Us

Information We Collect

When you register an account, submit a manuscript, or contact the Editorial Office through this site, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, institutional affiliation, and manuscript details.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to process manuscript submissions, coordinate peer review, communicate with authors and reviewers about the status of a submission, and respond to inquiries sent through the contact form.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or other tracking technologies. It does not track your browsing activity across this or other sites. If that changes in the future, this section will be updated to describe what is used and why.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Manuscript and author details are shared only with editors and reviewers as necessary to carry out the double-blind peer review process described in our Review Process.

Data Retention and Security

Submitted information is retained for as long as needed to support the editorial and publication process, and is stored with reasonable safeguards against unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting the Editorial Office.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Please check this page periodically for changes.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be directed to jtead@usa.edu.ph.
