Journal of Technology, Engineering, Architecture and Design (JTEAD)

JTEAD is an open-access journal publishing original research across engineering, architecture, design, and technology for sustainable development. At the institutional level, it features high-quality college thesis manuscripts and colloquium proceedings.

ABOUT THE JOURNAL

The Journal of Technology, Engineering, Architecture and Design (JTEAD) is the official research journal of the College of Technology, University of San Agustin. It serves as a platform for disseminating high-quality, innovative, and impactful research outputs from students, faculty, researchers, and practitioners.
